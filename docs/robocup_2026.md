## Event
- **RoboCup 2026**: 2026-06-30 to 2026-07-06  
- **Location**: Incheon, South Korea

## Humanoid Soccer League (HSL)
HSL is formed by unifying the former Humanoid League (HL) and Standard Platform League (SPL), and will be hosted for the first time at RoboCup 2026.

## Division constraints (Small)
- **Height < 110 cm**
- **Weight < 15 kg**

## Team configurations
Teams may choose one of two configurations:

- **Foundation**: fewer players
- **Advanced**: more players

If one team chooses Foundation, **both teams must play Foundation** for that game.

### Max players (Small)
- **Foundation: max 4**
- **Advanced: max 7**
