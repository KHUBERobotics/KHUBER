# Members

KHUBER is an interdisciplinary robotics team at **Kyung Hee University**,
bringing together expertise in **biomedical engineering, embedded systems,
perception, learning, and humanoid robot soccer**.

The team is organized with clear technical and operational roles to support
long-term development toward **RoboCup 2026**.

## Organization Philosophy

- Clear responsibility ownership (hardware, software, integration)
- Strong simulation–hardware feedback loop
- Documentation and reproducibility as first-class outputs
- Long-term preparation toward competition-level robustness
